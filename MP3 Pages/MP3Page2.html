Barry Manilow - Copacabana


First Heard: 200X

This song won a grammy for best pop singer, and it's not hard to see why! Barry Manilow's vocals bring a great energy and have such a fantastic flair. This song is an absolute blast to sing. Even my preteen nieces and nephew wanted to listen to it over and over again. Just remember: At the Copacabana, don't fall in love!
