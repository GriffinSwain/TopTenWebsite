Jamiroquai - Canned Heat


First Heard: Whenever I first watched Napoleon Dynamite

This song is a groove, full stop. This song is probably best known as the song that Napoleon Dynamite dances to at the climax of the film. At the time I thought it was a female singer, but that is not the case! I've memorized the lyrics to all the songs on this list, and this one was definitely the most challenging!
