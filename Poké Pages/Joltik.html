Joltik

I don't like bugs. I absolutely could not like ticks. But Joltik is an exception. He is just a fuzzy little guy who likes to drain power from buildings, electronics, and other electric type pokemon. For example, Yamper is unable to store the electricity it produces, so Joltik will stick to it and absorb the electricity as it is released.
