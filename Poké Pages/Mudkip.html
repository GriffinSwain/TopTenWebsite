10. Mudkip

Our final pokemon on the list. Mudkip is based on an axolotl, like another pokemon I listed before. He has a huge mouth but such a tiny body, and I just love him. I used him when playing Ruby/Sapphire, and again with the mystery dungeon games where he was available. Derpy little water guys are lovely.
